The data discovery loop

Raw CSVs land in the lake with no description attached. Discovery is the loop that gives them one: profile the data, infer its schema, and catalog it so every later service can read it. The catalog is never quite right on the first pass, so you come back around and correct the types.

The loop

A Glue crawler walks the raw prefix, samples the rows, guesses a schema, and writes it into the Glue Data Catalog. Inspect, correct, and the loop tightens.
