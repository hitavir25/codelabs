The six key functions of data engineering

Four functions flow in sequence as data moves from source to value. Two more wrap every stage: automate rides above, govern rides below. Color carries meaning here and across the whole track.

Automate — schedule and chain the steps so the pipeline runs on its own wraps every stage
1
Build infrastructure

Build the foundation

Stand up the S3 lake, identity, and compute the platform runs on.

2
Ingest

Bring data in

Pull from databases, files, APIs, and streams into the lake.

3
Prepare

Make it usable

Clean, validate, and reshape raw into analytics-ready data.

4
Catalog

Describe and find

Record what each dataset means so others discover and trust it.

Govern & secure — quality, least-privilege access, encryption, and audit, end to end wraps every stage

Color key — one hue, one concept, everywhere
